Site Context and References

This topic was introduced in NMED 3310 Game Design: Theory and Practice. I chose this topic as Satoru Iwata's influence on the gaming industry was a big part of my life as I enjoyed the video games he was a part in making throughout my childhood. This led to me wanting to pursue design and coding in my education, thus I chose this as my topic for this site.
